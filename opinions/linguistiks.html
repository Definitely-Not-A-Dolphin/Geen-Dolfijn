We need to add more letters to the English Alphabet

The English language is incredibly inconsistent with its spelling. To improve on this, I think that we should add new letters (from other languages or from the IPA) to English.

Lets start with the unvoiced dental fricative and the voiced dental fricative. The unvoiced dental fricative is the /th/-sound in "think", and "thin". We should replace this with the letter þorn (þ, Þ), which at one point was actually used in English until the days of the printing press. The voiced dental fricative is also a /th/-sound, but now as in "the" and "that". We can replace þis with the letter eð (ð, Ð). Boþ of ðese letters are still used in Icelandic, a in my opinion fairly underrated language.
